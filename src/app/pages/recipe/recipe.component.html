Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Slice the eggplant, zucchini, and bell peppers into thin rounds.
  3. Arrange the sliced vegetables in a circular pattern in a baking dish, alternating them and overlapping slightly.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with minced garlic, salt, pepper, and thyme.
  5. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 40 minutes.
  6. Uncover and bake for an additional 20 minutes, until the vegetables are tender.
  7. Serve warm, garnished with fresh thyme.
